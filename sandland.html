Cold Itzler

  • Press space to turn off the AI and assume control of the faction you are watching.
  • Drag to select your uints, command them with right click.
  • As humans. hold B and click a tile to build a wall, hold x and click a wall to dismantle it.
  • As humans. hold N and click a tile to build a barracks. Click it to select it to make units using the UI on the right.
  • As Cold Itzlerians. units can metamorphose, this is controlled by clicking the button in the UI on the right.
  • Press H and K alternatively to swap sides.
  • Humans will freeze to death without wind barrier walls.
  • The wind speed may increase, making survival difficult.
  • Cold Itzlerians are immune to cold.
  • Use the minimap to navigate the large map, or WASD.
  • Brown tiles are hotrock, you can harvest it with any unit by standing on the tile.
